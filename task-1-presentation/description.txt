My infographic was designed with Canva, and I found that it is very easy to use and friendly for newbies like me. Canva offers many templates and design elements that can be used to create an infographic. I decided to create an infographic on important aspects of digital literacy, such as safe online practices, email etiquette, professional online image, and useful digital tools.

My design is very simple and visually appealing because I used icons and colors to present my ideas. I used bullet points instead of paragraphs to ensure that my design is easy to comprehend. I added a definition and a quote to my design to ensure that I am conveying some meaning.

From this assignment, I have learned that presenting information is very important and can be very useful for boosting one's creativity and awareness of safe and ethical use of digital platforms.
