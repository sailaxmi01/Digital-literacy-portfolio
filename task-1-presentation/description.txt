I created my infographic using Canva, which I found very easy and convenient to use, especially as a beginner. It offers a variety of templates, icons, and design elements that helped me organize my ideas clearly. In my infographic, I focused on important aspects of digital literacy such as safe internet practices, email etiquette, maintaining a professional online presence, and using helpful digital tools.

While designing, I made sure to keep the layout simple and visually appealing by using icons, colours, and short points instead of long paragraphs. This helped in making the information easy to understand at a glance. I also added a short definition of digital literacy and a quote to make the content more meaningful.

Through this task, I learned how important it is to present information in a clear and engaging way. It improved my creativity and also increased my awareness about responsible and safe use of digital platforms in daily life.
