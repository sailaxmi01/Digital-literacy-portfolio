Emails or messages can sometimes be misinterpreted, which may lead to confusion or incorrect assumptions.

By drafting formal emails, I understood how essential it is to follow a proper structure, including a clear subject line, polite greeting, well-organized content, and a respectful closing. Using formal language and maintaining a professional tone helps in conveying the message clearly and creates a positive impression on the receiver.

Additionally, creating a social media etiquette checklist helped me understand responsible online behavior. It emphasized the importance of privacy, respectful interaction, and being cautious while sharing information online.

Overall, this task enhanced my communication skills and made me more aware of how to interact effectively and responsibly in digital environments, both academically and professionally.
